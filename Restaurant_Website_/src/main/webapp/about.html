About Us



Welcome to Calcutta 64, where culinary excellence meets unforgettable dining experiences. Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, our restaurant is a haven for food lovers, offering a perfect blend of tradition and innovation in every dish.


Our Story


Founded in 2008, Calcutta 64 is the brainchild of Subrata Ghosh, whose passion for gastronomy is reflected in every corner of our establishment. Inspired by cultural influences, travel experiences, family recipes, etc., our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the joy of exquisite, hand-crafted meals to our community.
